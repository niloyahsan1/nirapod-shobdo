About Nirapod Shobdo

First app in Bangladesh making legal rights accessible for people with disabilities

Nirapod Shobdo is a pioneering prototype application designed to provide rights information and reporting support for people with disabilities in Bangladesh. We focus on making legal rights truly accessible through multiple accessibility modes.

Our app serves Neurodevelopmental Disabilities (NDD) learners including individuals with autism, ADHD and intellectual disabilities as well as sensory learners with hearing and visual impairments. We also support families, caregivers and teachers in their journey toward justice and equality.

App Features

Multimodal accessibility and comprehensive rights reporting tools

Text Mode

Simple, easy to read Bangla text with large fonts and clear formatting for users who prefer reading content.

Audio Mode

Voice-based navigation and content delivery with audio buttons for all rights information and reporting options.

Sign Mode

Sign language support with video submissions and visual communication for deaf and hard of hearing users.

Visual Mode

Easy read interface with icons, visual cues and simplified content for users with intellectual disabilities.

Voice Reporting

Record voice messages to report cases, complaints or request help without needing to type or write.

Video Reporting

Upload sign language videos to communicate reports and requests (coming soon in future updates).

Icon-Based Forms

Visual complaint forms using icons and symbols for users who struggle with text-based interfaces.

Rights Information

Access details on Education Rights, Legal Protection, Disability Act 2013 and Protection from Abuse.

Tracking System

Get confirmation with Tracking ID for submitted reports and follow up on case status and progress.

Our Mission

To make legal rights truly accessible for people with Neurodevelopmental Disabilities (NDD) and Sensory Disabilities in Bangladesh through innovative multimodal technology, ensuring everyone can understand and exercise their legal rights.

Our Vision

A Bangladesh where disability is never a barrier to accessing justice and legal rights. We envision a future where legal information and reporting systems are fully accessible to all citizens aligning with the Smart Bangladesh Vision 2041.

What Makes Us Unique

First app in Bangladesh combining multimodal accessibility, Bangla-first civic technology, safe reporting mechanisms and education empowerment - all designed specifically for the disability community's legal needs.
